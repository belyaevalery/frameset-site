Market cycles of conviction

Built by founders, for founders.

Founded in 2019 by Vance Spencer and Michael Anderson — formerly of Netflix and Snapchat — Framework saw initial success as one of the first venture firms in the digital asset space.

The firm has since raised additional funds and expanded its focus toward emerging technologies including AI, infrastructure, and next-generation networks. Based in San Francisco, we take a high-conviction approach and usually lead early-stage rounds.

Our approach

Over four market cycles, we've become experts at discerning which founders have the grit, drive, and product-market fit to launch and scale a category-defining company.
